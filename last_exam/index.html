About us

Elena Tonra

vocals, guitar, bass

Igor Haefeli

guitar, bass

Remi Aguilella

drums, percussion

Daughter are an indie folk band from England. Fronted by North London native Elena Tonra
(born 15 January 1990), they were formed in 2010 after the addition of Swiss-born guitarist
Igor Haefeli and drummer Remi Aguilella from France. They have released four EPs, three
singles and three albums, and are currently signed to Glassnote (North America) and 4AD
(Europe). After playing the local London circuit, they toured supporting Ben Howard around
Europe and have since played headlining tours around North America, Europe and Australia.
